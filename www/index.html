About

Panic button is an initiative from Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) to service excellence and commitment to the safety of the citizen. Your panic alert will be received by nearest Police station and your profile contacts. Alert will contain you GPS location when available.

Disclaimer

Panic button is an initiative from Dhaka Metropolitan Police (DMP) to service excellence and commitment to the safety of the citizen. Your panic alert will be received by nearest Police station including the location. While DMP & GP provides its best effort, they will not be liable for any loss or damage in using this service. Users of the service are advised to take standard safe measures as usual.

Safety Tips

  • In case of fire outbreak :
    Ask for assistance by dialing 999 or the nearest Police Station phone number in case of sudden outbreak of fire.

  • Personal security :
    DMP provides security to the person who reasonably shows ground to be protected.

  • Money Escort:
    DMP provides Money Escort to citizens.

  • Assistance to the victim :
    Providing security and assistance to the victim of criminal case is the prime duty of DMP. Victim just needs to contact DMP for his/her protection. DMP personnel also mentor the victims of criminal cases in legal matters.
